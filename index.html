Graveyard Escaped

Move using WASD or arrow keys, look around using the mouse.

E - Pick up key / Open gate

+ - Increase movement speed

- - Decrease movement speed


Hit the num key or digit key to:

1 - Toggle flying

2 - Toggle collisions

3 - Toggle ambient light

4 - Toggle rain

5 - Toggle lightning

6 - Toggle infinite lightning

7 - Trigger a lightning strike

8 - Toggle wireframe

9 - Toggle this hint
